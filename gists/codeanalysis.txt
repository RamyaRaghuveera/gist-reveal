 <p style="text-align: left; font-family: IBM Plex Sans Bold; font-size: 50px;">SonarQube</p>
 <div style="display: flex;">
   <div style = "font-size: 20px; font-family:  IBM Plex Sans; text-align: left;">
      <ul style="line-height: 25px;line-break: auto;">
        <li>SonarQube is a open source tool built by SonarSource.</li><br>
        <li>It has a strong community of 120K users</li><br>
        <li>It supports 27 programming langauges.</li><br>
        <li>SonarQube plugs into the  application lifecycle management process to make continuous inspection part of continuous integration.</li><br>
        <li>The SonarQube solution consists of several components: </li><br>
        <li><b>SonarScanner</b>: The brain of SonarQube.It  processes the resulting analysis reports. </li><br>
        <li><b>SonarQube UI</b>: It displays the reports. </li><br>
        <li><b>SonarQube Database</b>: The reports are stored here. </li><br>
        <li><b>SonarQube Server</b>: The central component which runs the SonarScanner, stores the reports in SonarQube Database, and displays the reports in the SonarQube UI.</li><br>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
